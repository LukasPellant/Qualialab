3D world and controls
Renders a 64×64 ground with borders, lit scene, and performance stats.
Camera with orbit controls in God mode. Press F toggles mode label (Character mode is WIP). Press R resets the sandbox.
Seeded world
Starts with a townhall, farm, forest, mine, and 2 idle workers at defined positions.
UI/HUD
Top HUD shows tick, resources (wood/stone/food/gold), and population (idle/cap, recruit timer).
Build Menu (bottom-left): pick farm, mine, or house; shows costs and whether you can afford it.
Worker Panel (bottom-right): shows buildings, capacity/assigned; add/remove worker assignment per building. Town Hall dialog spawns a worker using 1 idle population.
Building placement
Select in Build Menu → green ghost preview → click ground to place if affordable.
Deducts resources on placement, spawns building, blocks that grid cell for pathfinding.
Workers and tasks
From the Worker Panel, assigning a worker sets a “gather” task to the chosen farm/mine/forest.
A* pathfinding on a 2D grid; workers move at ~1.5 m/s; per-frame movement smoothing; state transitions idle → moving → working.
On arrival, workers produce:
Forest: consumes stock.wood and adds wood.
Mine: adds stone.
Farm: adds food.
Workers remain at their assigned site and keep working in cycles.
Economy and population
Forest passively regrows wood stock up to a cap.
Food consumption per worker per second; farms add passive food while active to balance consumption.
Population cap from buildings: townhall +10, house +5.
Auto-recruit: if under cap and have ≥40 food and ≥10 gold, a 30s recruit starts; when done, +1 idle. Clicking Town Hall can convert idle → in-world worker.
